Robotic Process Automation

Robotic Process Automation is the use of software with artifical intelligence and machine learning capabilities that automates all the process workers do in their job filed. Every 15 minutes of human work equals to one minute of automation. It is more efficient and more accurate. An article explains that 92% of European Firms are going to adapt RPA by 2020 by the new survey from ISG. Companies that begin to adapt the RPA first will increase their revenue significantly, becoming more competitve in the market.

IT Corporations are attempting to develop their own RPA programming software and EDENTNS.INC is the Korean Company that has its own effective software.
